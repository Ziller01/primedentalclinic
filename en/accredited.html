General Authority for Healthcare Accreditation and Regulation (GAHAR)

We adhere to the quality and safety standards set by GAHAR, providing care that meets national accreditation guidelines focused on patient safety and service excellence.

Universal Health Insurance – Egypt

We are proud to be part of Egypt’s Universal Health Insurance system, allowing eligible patients to receive quality care with the support of the national health coverage program.

Dental Syndicate – Egypt

Our clinic is officially accredited by the Egyptian Dental Syndicate, ensuring that all dental care is provided by licensed professionals who meet national medical standards.

Ministry of Health and Population – Egypt

Our clinic is fully licensed by the Egyptian Ministry of Health and Population, ensuring compliance with all health and safety regulations for a secure and trusted dental environment.
